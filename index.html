New information package for responsible adults

The Australian Government is calling responsible adults to rise in the battle against misinformation to protect their loved ones and our society.

With the large amount of information released about COVID-19, it is common for adults and children to feel overwhelmed or anxious. Responsible adults have an important role to play in the battle against misinformation and the manipulation of the truth when discussing COVID-19. These individuals are in the important position to communicate information to their dependants effectively during these unprecedented times. Anyone is capable of spreading misinformation and the manipulation of truth that can potentially harm others whether it be intentional or not. Therefore, the Australian Government aims to provide responsible adults with the information and resources to effectively communicate in an age appropriate manner with their dependants.

This information package requires you to enter your dependants age groups. This information will not be stored and will only be used to enhance the package's ability to provide you information and advice that is relevant to your scenario.
